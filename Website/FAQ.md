# Frequently Asked Questions — Website

---

## What is the AnubisX Framework?

A scientific methodology for digital identity attribution based on invariant human cognitive signatures.

## How is it different?

Traditional attribution tracks what adversaries use (IPs, devices). AnubisX tracks who adversaries are — involuntary cognitive patterns they cannot change.

## What are the five modalities?

Stylometry (linguistic patterns), chrono-profiling (temporal patterns), terminal profiling (interaction patterns), network analysis (social patterns), and media forensics (environmental patterns).

## Is it implemented?

The framework is a complete theoretical specification. Software implementation is the next phase.

## What is Identity Intelligence?

A proposed formal forensic discipline for behavioral identity attribution.

## Is it peer reviewed?

The framework follows a formal publication pipeline with peer review. Planned publications will undergo standard scientific peer review.

## How is confidence quantified?

Attribution strength is expressed as a Likelihood Ratio — the probability of the evidence under same-source vs. different-source hypotheses.

## What about privacy?

The Proportionality and Privacy Protection Protocol (P³) is embedded in the core architecture, ensuring ethical constraints on attribution methods.

## Who should use this?

Forensic investigators, cybersecurity analysts, academic researchers, legal professionals, and policy makers.
