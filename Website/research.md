# Research

**AnubisX Framework — Scientific Publications and Research**

---

## Publication Roadmap

The framework's research is organized across five planned publications:

| Paper | Title | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Paper 01 | AnubisX Framework Introduction | Overview, architecture, conceptual model |
| Paper 02 | Theoretical Foundations | Axiomatic system, identity theory |
| Paper 03 | Mathematical Framework | Formal definitions, function taxonomy, domain theory |
| Paper 04 | Algorithmic Implementation | Behavioral signal processing, attribution algorithms |
| Paper 05 | Validation and Benchmarking | Empirical validation methodology, acceptance criteria |

## Case Studies

Case studies across multiple domains demonstrate the framework's application:

- **Social Media**: Stylometric and chrono profiling on social media platforms
- **Rich Social Environments**: Multi-modal attribution in diverse social contexts
- **Cross-Platform**: Identity resolution across different platforms
- **Specialized Scenarios**: Edge conditions and specialized use cases

## Experiments

A comprehensive set of experiments across multiple domains provides the empirical basis for validation:

- Platform-specific experiments
- Cross-platform experiments
- Benchmark experiments
- Ablation studies
- Stress tests
