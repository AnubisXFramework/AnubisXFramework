## Brand Guidelines

### Tone
- Scientific, rigorous, and precise
- Accessible to educated non-specialists
- Confident but not overstated
- Transparent about limitations and uncertainties

### Description for Media (25 words)

> A scientific framework that transforms digital attribution by tracking persistent human cognitive signatures instead of transient technical artifacts.

### Description for Media (50 words)

> The AnubisX Framework introduces a paradigm shift in digital identity attribution — from technical to human-centric. By measuring five independent behavioral modalities through rigorous mathematical methods, it establishes Identity Intelligence as a formal forensic discipline with quantified uncertainty and Daubert-compliant standards.

## Key Messages

1. **Paradigm shift**: From technical artifacts to human cognitive signatures
2. **Scientific discipline**: Establishing Identity Intelligence alongside DNA, fingerprints, ballistics
3. **Multi-modal**: Five independent behavioral signals fused for high-confidence attribution
4. **Quantified**: Every conclusion includes measured uncertainty
5. **Ethical**: Privacy, proportionality, and dual-use safeguards built into the architecture

## Talking Points

### For Scientific Audiences

- A formal axiomatic system establishing behavioral identity as a rigorous discipline
- The Cognitive Centroid as a mathematically defined attractor
- Empirical validation methodology with Daubert-compliant standards
- Multi-modal fusion using tensor decomposition and evidence theory

### For Industry Audiences

- Shifts attribution from spoofable technical indicators to persistent human signatures
- Five independent signals make counter-forensics exponentially harder
- Quantified confidence supports better operational decision-making
- Open academic license for non-commercial research

### For General Audiences

- Your behavior online is as unique as your fingerprint
- The way you type, write, and interact creates a pattern that identifies you
- This makes it possible to identify anonymous actors even when they hide their technology
- Built with privacy protections and ethical safeguards
