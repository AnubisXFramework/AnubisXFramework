## Tone Guidelines (All Posts)

- **Professional**: Scientific register, no promotional claims
- **Measured**: Every claim qualified with its current status
- **Transparent**: Limitations stated alongside contributions
- **No overselling**: Accurately distinguish validated capabilities from theoretical claims

## Required Disclaimers

Every post in this campaign should carry (explicitly or implicitly) the following message: *The AnubisX Framework is a complete theoretical specification. Not all claims are fully validated — see the limitations documentation for details.*

## Key Messages (Consistent Across Posts)

1. The framework shifts attribution from technical artifacts to human cognitive signatures
2. It is a complete specification — axioms, mathematics, algorithms, validation, benchmarks, case studies
3. It is open for collaboration, review, and implementation
4. It is licensed CC BY 4.0 for academic and research use
