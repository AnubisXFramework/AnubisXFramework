# LinkedIn Campaign — Post 5: Innovation Highlights

**Type**: Innovation showcase
**Tone**: Substantive, specific, evidence-linked
**Target audience**: CTOs, CSOs, innovation officers, technology strategists

---

The AnubisX Framework introduces several innovations in the science of digital attribution. Each is documented in the repository with formal definitions.

**1. Cognitive Centroid Theory**

The first formalization of behavioral identity as an asymptotic attractor in feature space. The Cognitive Centroid is defined as the expected value of an individual's behavioral feature distribution. Convergence is governed by theoretical properties of the framework.

**Status**: Internal theoretical construct. Empirically testable through designed experiments.

**2. Involuntary Signal Hierarchy**

A formal ranking of behavioral features by resistance to counter-forensic manipulation. Core involuntary signals (function-word frequencies, inter-event timing distributions, error recovery patterns) are theoretically resistant to conscious modification. Levels range from core involuntary (highest resistance) to preference-based (lowest resistance).

**Status**: Theoretical proposition requiring empirical validation.

**3. Multi-Modal Fusion Comparison**

Multiple fusion strategies are specified within a single architecture — score-level, feature-level, and decision-level — enabling future empirical comparison of fusion effectiveness for behavioral data.

**Status**: Specified but not empirically compared.

**4. Systematic Error Decomposition**

Total error is decomposed into multiple components: measurement, context, estimation, temporal, and method. Error categories are specified with root cause analysis protocols.

**Status**: Design specification. Protocols pending empirical validation.

**5. Multi-Tier Validation with Quantified Criteria**

Acceptance thresholds across four tiers — from unit-level numerical stability to system-level operational performance. All thresholds were specified before implementation.

**Status**: Provisional design targets pending empirical calibration.

**6. Complete Algorithm Catalog**

Algorithm specifications with full traceability to axioms, equations, and validation criteria. Each specifies inputs, outputs, mathematical functions, dependencies, and acceptance criteria.

**Status**: Specified but not implemented.

---

**What these innovations share**: They are theoretical contributions. They are fully specified but untested empirically. The next phase is implementation and validation.
