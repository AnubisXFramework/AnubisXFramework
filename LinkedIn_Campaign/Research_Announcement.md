**Headline: AnubisX — An Open Research Platform for Behavioral Identity Attribution**

We are releasing the AnubisX Framework as an open research platform and invite the academic community to participate in its validation, extension, and application.

## What We're Releasing

The AnubisX Framework is a multi-modal behavioral identity attribution system. The release includes:

- A complete theoretical specification defining analytical layers, fusion stages, and a cross-platform adaptation architecture
- An operational prototype implementing the stylometric modality with validated fingerprint extraction
- Experimental results from proof-of-concept experiments
- Full documentation of capabilities, limitations, and research status

## Invitation for Peer Review

We consider this a living research artifact and actively invite methodological critique. Specific areas where peer review would be most valuable:

1. **Feature engineering decisions** — The choice and implementation of stylometric features, including lexical weighting schemes and syntactic markers
2. **Similarity metric choice** — Alternative metrics may yield different results
3. **Statistical validation** — Formal hypothesis testing with expanded samples is needed
4. **Linguistic analysis methodology** — Baseline approaches could be compared

## Open Collaboration Areas

### 1. Model Ensemble
A multi-model fusion layer is defined but requires implementation. We need collaborators to implement and evaluate this layer.

### 2. Temporal Analysis
We require time-stamped data collection to extract temporal features including posting periodicity, inter-message intervals, and circadian rhythm patterns. Open questions include the stability of temporal features relative to stylometric features.

### 3. Graph Analysis
Social graph construction from interaction networks is specified but unimplemented. We seek collaborators working on coordination detection and graph-based attribution.

### 4. Multi-Platform Evaluation
The platform adapter architecture is designed for extensibility. We are actively seeking partners to implement adapters for other platforms and evaluate cross-platform fingerprint portability.

### 5. Large-Scale Benchmarking
Systematic evaluation at scale with labeled ground truth is the critical path toward forensic application.
